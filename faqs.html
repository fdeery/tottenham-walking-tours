Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: May 2025

Where is the meeting point?

Outside Tottenham Hotspur Experience.

Is this a tour about Tottenham Hotspur Football Club?

No, we just start at the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium because it is a good meeting point.

How long is the tour (time)?

Two hours, with a 15 minute break half-way through. The tour therefore lasts for two hours and 15 minutes in total.

How far will we walk in total (distance)?

3.3 miles / 5.31 km, on mostly flat terrain. There will be one small hill.

Will there be any breaks?

Yes, one break of 15 minutes, during which you can sit down, get a coffee, use the bathroom, etc.

What should I wear?

Anything that you feel comfortable walking 3.3 miles in!

Can I take photos?

Yes, provided you have the permission of anyone who is going to be in them.

Where does the tour finish?

Outside The High Cross Pub. Note that this is *not* the same place that the tour starts, but is on the same street and within sight of the stadium.
