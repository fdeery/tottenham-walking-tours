Private Tours

Perhaps the public tour times don't suit you, or you're looking for an even more intimate or tailored experience? Where possible, we'd love to accommodate your request for a private tour.

Under normal circumstances:

  • A standard private tour currently costs £120 for four guests or less;
  • A standard private tour currently costs £30 per person for five guests or more;
  • We are open to providing custom private tours tailored to tour groups' specific interests (e.g. specific communities within the area, food, Tottenham Hostpur FC);
  • Our public liability license currently prohibits us from accommodating groups of more than 15 walking tourists.
